PIG

Here are 7 key points to explain the code:

1. **Rolling Dice Function:** The `roll()` function generates a random number between 1 and 6, simulating a dice roll.

2. **Player Count Input:** The program prompts the user to input the number of players (between 2 and 4) and validates the input to ensure it's within the allowed range.

3. **Initialize Scores:** The program creates a list `player_scores` to track each player's score, initialized to 0 for all players.

4. **Gameplay Loop:** The main game loop continues until one player's score reaches or exceeds the target score of 50 points.

5. **Player's Turn:** During each player's turn, they decide whether to roll the dice or end their turn. If they roll a 1, their turn ends and they score 0 for that round. Otherwise, their roll value is added to their current score.

6. **Updating Scores:** After each player's turn, their accumulated score for that round is added to their total score.

7. **Determine Winner:** Once a player's score reaches 50 or more, the game ends, and the player with the highest score is declared the winner.
